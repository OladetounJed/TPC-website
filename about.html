About Us

Just one visit will change your life.

OUR MAIN GOALS

Our main goal at the Power Stream Church is to make Jesus and his power known through his word. Then we leave him to do the rest. We minister to the total man, we cut across every sphere of human endeavour.

OUR CHILDREN

Our children maintain the same standard of excellence as the adult church. We raise children for the next generation, we are surrounded with good environment of worship, caring with competent children teachers.

OUR SERVICES

You are welcome to have a first hand personal encounter with the living God as you join us in any of our services. It’s always a wonderful experience and a celebration time in God’s presence.

OUR MISSION

We plant churches strategically and systematically.

OUR VISION

To mature You to a highest level of distinction in life so as to impact your world with your greatness and influence.

OUR CORE VALUES

We are motivated by four things at TPC. They are RIGHTEOUSNESS,EXCELLENCE, LOVE and SERVICE. Righteousness is a gift that comes to us by Jesus and we are not righteous by works, We are justified by faith. We strive for excellence because it is the description of our God. We aspire for love because it’s the only force that conquers. Conclusively, service is at the tips of our assignment on earth.
We were saved to serve humanity so that people will come to the saving knowledge of God.

Our Pastor

Kolawole A. Olaoye is the president of the Sought Watchers Int’l Ministries, popularly known as SOWIN Ministries. He’s the founder and senior pastor of The Powerstream Church, a multi-racial, multi-tribal and multi-faceted church with branches all over the country and outside the country. He’s called to preach relationship with God and to God’s people through the preaching of the message of faith. He’s happily married to Olufunke which happens to be his strong meet in the ministry and together they have three lovely children, Zenas Modefoluwa, Doxa Modebareoluwa and Zoan Moboluwarin.
